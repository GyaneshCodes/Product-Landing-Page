Yamaha P-125 Digital Piano

Features

    Authentic Sound & Feel:

  • Features Yamaha's Pure CF Sound Engine, which replicates the rich tones of a grand piano.
  • 88 fully weighted keys with Graded Hammer Standard (GHS) technology for a realistic piano feel.

    Smart & Portable Design:

  • Compact and lightweight, making it easy to move and store.
  • Sleek, modern design that fits any home or stage setup.

    Built-in Rhythms & Effects:

  • Comes with 20 different rhythms (pop, jazz, rock, etc.) to enhance practice and performance.
  • Reverb, chorus, and other sound effects to customize your playing style..

    Recording & Connectivity:

  • USB to Host function lets you connect to a computer or smartphone for MIDI recording.
  • Compatible with Yamaha's Smart Pianist App, allowing easy control of sounds and settings.

    Built-in Speakers & Headphone Output:

  • High-quality stereo speakers for a full, rich sound.
  • 3.5mm headphone jack for silent practice sessions.

How It Works

Turn It On & Choose a Sound:

Power up the piano and select your preferred sound from multiple instrument voices (Grand Piano, Electric Piano, Strings, etc.).

Adjust Touch & Effects:

Modify the touch sensitivity and add effects like reverb to personalize your playing experience.

Practice with Built-in Rhythms:

Use the built-in drum rhythms to keep time and enhance your practice.

Record & Connect to Apps:

Record your performance or connect the piano to apps like GarageBand or Yamaha Smart Pianist for digital composing and learning.

Enjoy Anytime, Anywhere:

Play through built-in speakers or plug in headphones for private practice.
